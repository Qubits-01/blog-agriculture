Climate Change is Around the Corner.
We Need to Change Now!
Randa Ancheta   September 24, 2019

   Developing countries in Asia and the Pacific are likely to face the highest reductions in agricultural potential in the world due to climate change. As a result, climate change will place an additional burden on the region, which could undermine its efforts to meet long-term development goals.

Adapt to the change!

   We can create a genetically modified organism that would survive in conditions that seem to grow harsher and harsher by the day.

   We can also adapt to the process of vertical farming. This would be an indoor process wherein agriculture goes up instead of wide. Soil is actually not needed in this. Only water and air would be enough. All variables must be controlled to ensure the success of implementation.

   We can also do something to the biofuels and biomass so that the production of greenhouse gases can be reduced. These biofuels can be converted to renewable energy.
